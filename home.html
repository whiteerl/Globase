Welcome

Welcome to the homepage for GLOBASE Guatemala. Our organization strives to connect talented MBA students to global consulting opportunities, as they serve real clients on short-term engagements abroad.



How it Works

Selected first-year MBA students are invited to participate in a short-term engagement in Guatemala after submitting an application. Each consulting team is assigned a member of the GLOBASE Leadership Team, which consists of second-year students with a passion for consulting and travel who are responsible for organizing the overall experience.

Apply Today

Interested participants are encouraged to apply to the GLOBASE Guatemala program early, as spots often fill quickly. Please navigate to the student application page to take the initial step!
